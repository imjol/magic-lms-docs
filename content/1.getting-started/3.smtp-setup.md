# SMTP Setup

***


- **Login in to you cpanel webmail and scroll down to "Mail Client Manual Settings"**
:nuxt-img{alt="Webmail Dashboard" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/SMTP/Step1.png"}


- **Go to your application and scroll down to "Setting" option and click on it**
:nuxt-img{alt="MagicLMS Dashboard" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/SMTP/Step2.png"}


- **Click on SMTP Settings**
:nuxt-img{alt="MagicLMS Dashboard" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/SMTP/Step3.png"}


- **Select SMTP from the "MAIL DRIVER" section**
:nuxt-img{alt="MagicLMS Dashboard" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/SMTP/Step4.png"}


- **Click on MAIL HOST**
:nuxt-img{alt="MagicLMS Dashboard" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/SMTP/Step5.png"}


- **Click on SMTP Port and Copy "465"**
:nuxt-img{alt="MagicLMS Dashboard" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/SMTP/Step6.png"}


- **Type "465"**
:nuxt-img{alt="MagicLMS Dashboard" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/SMTP/Step7.png"}

- **Click on MAIL_USERNAME and Type youe Username**
:nuxt-img{alt="MagicLMS Dashboard" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/SMTP/Step8.png"}

- **Click on password field**
:nuxt-img{alt="MagicLMS Dashboard" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/SMTP/Step9.png"}


- **Type "ssl"**
:nuxt-img{alt="MagicLMS Dashboard" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/SMTP/Step10.png"}


- **Type "yourmail@mail.com"**
:nuxt-img{alt="MagicLMS Dashboard" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/SMTP/Step11.png"}


- **Type "Your application name"**
:nuxt-img{alt="MagicLMS Dashboard" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/SMTP/Step12.png"}


- **Click on Save**
:nuxt-img{alt="MagicLMS Dashboard" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/SMTP/Step13.png"}
