# Zoom application Setup


### Setting up zoom in "zoom app marketplace"

- **Click on Develop**
:nuxt-img{alt="zoom applciation marketplace dashboard" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/zoom-app-marketplace/Step1.png"}


- **Click on Build App**
:nuxt-img{alt="zoom applciation marketplace dashboard" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/zoom-app-marketplace/Step01.png"}


- **Select Server to Server OAuth App**
:nuxt-img{alt="zoom applciation marketplace dashboard" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/zoom-app-marketplace/Step2.png"}


- **Type "your application name"**
:nuxt-img{alt="zoom applciation marketplace dashboard" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/zoom-app-marketplace/Step3.png"}


- **Click on create**
:nuxt-img{alt="zoom applciation marketplace app dashboard" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/zoom-app-marketplace/Step4.png"}


- **Click "Continue" – (We'll be back to this page soon!)**
:nuxt-img{alt="zoom applciation marketplace credentials page" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/zoom-app-marketplace/Step5.png"}


- **Next, you'll be taken to the "App Credentials" page. Here, enter "your app name", a short description of your application**
:nuxt-img{alt="zoom applciation marketplace credential page" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/zoom-app-marketplace/Step6.png"}


- **Type "your company name" and scroll down**
:nuxt-img{alt="zoom applciation marketplace credential page" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/zoom-app-marketplace/Step7.png"}


- **Type "your name"**
:nuxt-img{alt="zoom applciation marketplace credential page" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/zoom-app-marketplace/Step8.png"}


- **Type "your mail address"**
:nuxt-img{alt="zoom applciation marketplace credential page" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/zoom-app-marketplace/Step9.png"}

- **Click on Continue**
:nuxt-img{alt="zoom applciation marketplace credential page" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/zoom-app-marketplace/Step10.png"}


- **Click on "Add Scopes**
:nuxt-img{alt="zoom applciation marketplace credential page" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/zoom-app-marketplace/Step11.png"}


- **Click on Meeting from the left side then select "View all user meetings**
:nuxt-img{alt="zoom applciation marketplace credential page" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/zoom-app-marketplace/Step13.png"}


- **Select "View a user's meetings"**
:nuxt-img{alt="zoom applciation marketplace credential page" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/zoom-app-marketplace/Step14.png"}


- **Go back and find "View and manage all user meetings" and click on it**
:nuxt-img{alt="zoom applciation marketplace credential page" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/zoom-app-marketplace/Step15.png"}

- **Scroll down slowly and find "Create a meeting for a user" and select it.**
:nuxt-img{alt="zoom applciation marketplace credential page" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/zoom-app-marketplace/Step16.png"}

- **Click on Done**
:nuxt-img{alt="zoom applciation marketplace credential page" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/zoom-app-marketplace/Step17.png"}


- **Click on Continue**
:nuxt-img{alt="zoom applciation marketplace credential page" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/zoom-app-marketplace/Step18.png"}


- **Finally click on "Activate your app"**
:nuxt-img{alt="zoom applciation marketplace credential page" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/zoom-app-marketplace/Step19.png"}

:nuxt-img{alt="zoom applciation marketplace credential page" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/zoom-magiclms-setup/Step1.png"}


- **Click on Account ID Copy**
:nuxt-img{alt="zoom applciation marketplace credential page" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/zoom-magiclms-setup/Step2.png"}




- **Go back to your website. Paste the copied content into Zoom setup -> Zoom account ID**
:nuxt-img{alt="MagicLMS Zoom meeting" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/zoom-magiclms-setup/Step3.png"}


- **Click on Client ID copy:**
:nuxt-img{alt="zoom applciation marketplace credential page" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/zoom-magiclms-setup/Step4.png"}


- **Go back to your website. Paste the copied content into Zoom setup -> Zoom Client ID**

- **Copy Client Secret**
:nuxt-img{alt="zoom applciation marketplace credential page" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/zoom-magiclms-setup/Step6.png"}

### Creating a New Meeting

- **Click on Create Meeting**
:nuxt-img{alt="MagicLMS Zoom meeting" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/create-meeting/Step1.png"}


- **Select one of the class from the selection menu**
:nuxt-img{alt="MagicLMS Zoom meeting" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/create-meeting/Step2.png"}


- **Type a meeting topic (Mandatory)**
:nuxt-img{alt="MagicLMS Zoom meeting" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/create-meeting/Step3.png"}


- **Select either toggle option for "Recurring Meeting" or you can setup your meeting time from the below tab by assigning specific date & time**
:nuxt-img{alt="MagicLMS Zoom meeting" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/create-meeting/Step4.png"}


- **Type your Meeting Agenda (Mandatory)**
:nuxt-img{alt="MagicLMS Zoom meeting" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/create-meeting/Step5.png"}


- **Select your timezone from the "Timezone" tab**
:nuxt-img{alt="MagicLMS Zoom meeting" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/create-meeting/Step6.png"}



- **Enable Meeting settings with your own accord**
:nuxt-img{alt="MagicLMS Zoom meeting" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/create-meeting/Step7.png"}



- **Click on "Create Meeting"**
:nuxt-img{alt="MagicLMS Zoom meeting" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/create-meeting/Step8.png"}

### How to add zoom meeting as a class content


- **Click on Courses**
:nuxt-img{alt="MagicLMS Dashboard" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/zoom-meeting-class-content/Step1.png"}


- **Click on All Courses**
:nuxt-img{alt="MagicLMS Dashboard" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/zoom-meeting-class-content/Step2.png"}


- **Click on one of the created courses from "All courses" list**
:nuxt-img{alt="MagicLMS Courses Panel" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/zoom-meeting-class-content/Step3.png"}


- **Click on Add Zoom Meeting**
:nuxt-img{alt="MagicLMS Courses Panel" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/zoom-meeting-class-content/Step4.png"}

- **Select class from the tab**
:nuxt-img{alt="MagicLMS Courses Panel" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/zoom-meeting-class-content/Step5.png"}


- **Type "Beginning of the course"**
:nuxt-img{alt="MagicLMS Courses Panel" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/zoom-meeting-class-content/Step5.png"}

- **Check Recurring Metting (with no fixed time) if you don;t want to select a specific time**
:nuxt-img{alt="MagicLMS Courses Panel" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/zoom-meeting-class-content/Step6.png"}


- **Select a specific data and time as per your requirements**
:nuxt-img{alt="MagicLMS Courses Panel" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/zoom-meeting-class-content/Step7.png"}


- **Enter a time duration if you select a specific date & time**
:nuxt-img{alt="MagicLMS Dashboard" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/zoom-meeting-class-content/Step8.png"}


- **Passsword entry is optional.**
:nuxt-img{alt="MagicLMS Courses Panel" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/zoom-meeting-class-content/Step9.png"}

- **Type your meeting agenda**
:nuxt-img{alt="MagicLMS Courses Panel" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/zoom-meeting-class-content/Step10.png"}


- **Select your timezone from "TimeZone Tab""**
:nuxt-img{alt="MagicLMS Courses Panel" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/zoom-meeting-class-content/Step11.png"}


- **Enable Meeting settings with your own accord**
:nuxt-img{alt="MagicLMS Courses Panel" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/zoom-meeting-class-content/Step12.png"}


- **Select "Create Meeting"**
:nuxt-img{alt="MagicLMS Courses Panel" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/zoom-meeting-class-content/Step13.png"}
