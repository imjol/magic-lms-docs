# Payment Setup

##### MagicLMS offers a seamless and secure payment integration to help you monetize your courses effortlessly. This section will guide you through setting up payment gateways ensuring a smooth experience for both you and your learners.

### Stripe 


- **Log in to your Stripe account.**

- **Click on Publishible key**
:nuxt-img{alt="Stripe Dashboard API key" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/Stripe/Step1.png"}


- **Paste selected text into "STRIPE KEY" field**
:nuxt-img{alt="MagicLMS Payment Gateaway Settings" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/Stripe/Step2.png"}


- **Click on secret key**
:nuxt-img{alt="Stripe Dashboard API key" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/Stripe/Step3.png"}


- **Paste selected text into "STRIPE SECRET" field**
:nuxt-img{alt="MagicLMS Payment Gateaway Settings" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/Stripe/Step4.png"}


- **Click on Save**
:nuxt-img{alt="MagicLMS Payment Gateaway Settings" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/Stripe/Step5.png"}

<br/>



### Paypal

***

- **Log in to your Paypal account.**

- **Click on highlight**
:nuxt-img{alt="Paypal Dashboard" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/Paypal/Step1.png"}


- **Click on Apps & Credentials**
:nuxt-img{alt="Paypal Dashboard" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/Paypal/Step2.png"}


- **Click on the copy option for copying your desired Client ID**
:nuxt-img{alt="Paypal Dashboard" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/Paypal/Step3.png"}


- **Paste the copied content into "PAYPAL CLIENT ID" field**
:nuxt-img{alt="Paypal Dashboard" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/Paypal/Step4.png"}


- **Copy the secret content into your clipboard**
:nuxt-img{alt="Paypal Dashboard" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/Paypal/Step5.png"}


- **Paste copied content into "PAYPAL APP SECRET" field**
:nuxt-img{alt="Paypal Dashboard" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/Paypal/Step6.png"}


- **Click on Save**
:nuxt-img{alt="Paypal Dashboard" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/Paypal/Step7.png"}
