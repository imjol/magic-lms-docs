# Banner Content

🖼️ Banner Content – Customizable Visuals

The Banner Content feature in magicLMS allows admins to personalize homepage banners with custom images, text, and call-to-action buttons. Highlight important announcements, promotions, or key features to engage users effectively. With easy customization options, you can create visually appealing banners that enhance your platform’s look and feel.

- **Click on Banner Contents**
:nuxt-img{alt="banner content edit" src="https://imjol.b-cdn.net/MagicLMS/Docs/banner%20content/Step1.png"}



- **Click on dropdown trigger**
:nuxt-img{alt="banner content edit" src="https://imjol.b-cdn.net/MagicLMS/Docs/banner%20content/Step2.png"}


- **Click on Edit**
:nuxt-img{alt="banner content edit" src="https://imjol.b-cdn.net/MagicLMS/Docs/banner%20content/Step3.png"}


- **Edit your banner Title**
:nuxt-img{alt="banner content edit" src="https://imjol.b-cdn.net/MagicLMS/Docs/banner%20content/Step4.png"}


- **Edit your banner "Highlighted Title"**
:nuxt-img{alt="banner content edit" src="https://imjol.b-cdn.net/MagicLMS/Docs/banner%20content/Step5.png"}


- **Type your Sub Title**
:nuxt-img{alt="banner content edit" src="https://imjol.b-cdn.net/MagicLMS/Docs/banner%20content/Step6.png"}


- **Click on Upload From Media**
:nuxt-img{alt="banner content edit" src="https://imjol.b-cdn.net/MagicLMS/Docs/banner%20content/Step7.png"}


- **Select the image for "Banner Thumbnail"**
:nuxt-img{alt="banner content edit" src="https://imjol.b-cdn.net/MagicLMS/Docs/banner%20content/Step8.png"}


- **Click on Save**
:nuxt-img{alt="banner content edit" src="https://imjol.b-cdn.net/MagicLMS/Docs/banner%20content/Step9.png"}


- **Click on the toggle to publish the banner**
:nuxt-img{alt="banner content edit" src="https://imjol.b-cdn.net/MagicLMS/Docs/banner%20content/Step10.png"}
