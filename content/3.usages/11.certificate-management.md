# Certificate Management 

🏆 Certificate Settings – Reward Achievements

With Certificate Settings in magicLMS, easily design and customize certificates for course completions. Add your branding, instructor signatures, and issue certificates automatically—giving learners a tangible recognition of their success! 🎓✨

- **Click on Certificate Setting**
:nuxt-img{alt="certificate manager" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/certificate%20settings/Step1.png"}


- **Click on top_text to add top text**
:nuxt-img{alt="certificate manager" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/certificate%20settings/Step2.png"}


- **Click on header_text to add a header**
:nuxt-img{alt="certificate manager" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/certificate%20settings/Step3.png"}


- **Click on footer_text to add footer**
:nuxt-img{alt="certificate manager" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/certificate%20settings/Step4.png"}


- **Click on template_text and add your template text**
:nuxt-img{alt="certificate manager" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/certificate%20settings/Step5.png"}


- **Select YES or NO to generate a Barcode for certificate**
:nuxt-img{alt="certificate manager" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/certificate%20settings/Step6.png"}


- **Select a badge for the certificate**
:nuxt-img{alt="certificate manager" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/certificate%20settings/Step7.png"}


- **Select a logo badge for the certificate**
:nuxt-img{alt="certificate manager" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/certificate%20settings/Step8.png"}


- **Select a sample design image for the certificate**
:nuxt-img{alt="certificate manager" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/certificate%20settings/Step9.png"}


- **Click on Save**
:nuxt-img{alt="certificate manager" src="https://cdn.imjol.com/MagicLMS/Docs/certificate%20settings/Step10.png"}
